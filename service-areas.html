SERVICE AREAS IN ROCHESTER, NY

Serving Your Community

ROCHESTER'S TRUSTED LANDSCAPE PARTNER

Headquartered at 2565 Buffalo Road in Rochester, Westside Professional Landscape has been designing, building, and maintaining outdoor spaces across the Greater Rochester area for over 26 years. Our crews work in every corner of Monroe County — and we're familiar with the unique soil, drainage, and climate conditions in each community we serve.

Communities We Serve

OUR SERVICE AREAS

ROCHESTER

Headquarters

Our home base since 2000. We serve residential neighborhoods and commercial properties throughout the City of Rochester and its surrounding areas — from the 19th Ward to Charlotte, Park Avenue to Irondequoit.

PITTSFORD

One of our most active service areas. Pittsford's established neighborhoods and historic village homes demand premium landscape care, and we deliver — from estate-level design to weekly maintenance programs.

WEBSTER

Webster's mix of newer developments and established neighborhoods keeps our crews busy with landscape installations, lawn care programs, and seasonal maintenance throughout the town and village.

PENFIELD

From Four Corners to Panorama Trail, Penfield homeowners trust Westside for everything from landscape design and hardscaping to our 5-step lawn care program and seasonal cleanups.

FAIRPORT

Fairport's charming village center and surrounding residential areas are well within our service range. We handle landscape maintenance, design projects, and lawn care for homes along the Erie Canal and beyond.

BRIGHTON

Brighton's tree-lined streets and mature properties are ideal for our full range of services. From modernizing aging landscapes to maintaining established gardens, we're a trusted partner for Brighton homeowners.

GREECE

As one of Monroe County's largest towns, Greece offers a broad range of commercial and residential properties. We provide snow management for commercial clients and full landscape services for homeowners throughout the area.

GATES

Right next door to our Buffalo Road headquarters, Gates is one of our closest service areas. Whether it's a new patio installation, seasonal lawn care, or snow management, our crews are never far away.

CHILI

Chili's rural and suburban properties benefit from our full suite of services. Larger lots often need both landscape design and ongoing maintenance — and we handle both from start to finish.

HENRIETTA

Henrietta's commercial corridors and residential neighborhoods both fall within our service territory. We manage landscape maintenance and snow removal for retail centers, office parks, and HOAs in the area.

VICTOR

Victor's growing community of upscale residential developments and commercial properties makes it a natural fit for our design, installation, and maintenance services. Located just east of Monroe County in Ontario County.

SPENCERPORT

Spencerport homeowners along the Erie Canal and surrounding neighborhoods rely on Westside for landscape maintenance, lawn care, and seasonal services. A short drive west of our headquarters.
