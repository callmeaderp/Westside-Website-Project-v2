Who We Are

ROCHESTER'S PREMIER LANDSCAPE PARTNER

Westside Professional Landscape was founded in 2000 with a simple mission: to provide the Greater Rochester area with exceptional landscape services built on quality craftsmanship, honest communication, and genuine care for every property we touch.

What started as a small operation has grown into a full-service landscape company offering everything from custom design and installation to year-round maintenance, plant health programs, hardscaping, water features, snow management, and holiday decorating.

Today, we serve hundreds of residential and commercial clients across the Greater Rochester area, and we bring the same dedication and attention to detail to every single project — whether it's a small garden bed refresh or a complete property transformation.

Our Journey

26+ YEARS OF GREEN LANDSCAPES

2000
Founded in Rochester
Westside Professional Landscape is established, serving residential clients in the western Rochester suburbs with landscape design, installation, and maintenance.
2005
Expanded to Commercial Services
Growing demand from HOAs and commercial properties leads to expansion of services including large-scale maintenance contracts and snow management.
2010
Hardscaping Division Launched
Addition of dedicated hardscaping crews enables full-service outdoor living projects — patios, walls, walkways, and water features.
2018
Holiday Decorating Services
Launch of professional holiday decorating division, providing design, installation, and removal of custom holiday lighting and displays.
2023
Top 100 Recognition
Recognized among the industry's Top 100 landscape companies — a testament to our team's dedication to quality and growth.
2026
Plant Health & Lawn Care Division
Launch of the 5-Step Lawn Care Program and dedicated Plant Health division with NYS DEC Certified Applicators, bringing comprehensive turf management to our service portfolio.
