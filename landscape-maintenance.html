Protect Your Investment

PROFESSIONAL LANDSCAPE CARE

A beautiful landscape is an investment — and like any investment, it needs ongoing care to maintain its value. Without regular maintenance, plantings become overgrown, lawns thin out, beds fill with weeds, and the curb appeal you worked hard to create gradually disappears.

Westside Professional Landscape provides comprehensive maintenance programs for residential and commercial properties throughout the Greater Rochester area. Our trained crews deliver consistent, high-quality service on a predictable schedule — so your property always looks its best, whether it's a private home in Pittsford or a commercial campus in Henrietta.

What's Included

MAINTENANCE SERVICES

Comprehensive care programs tailored to your property's needs — from weekly mowing to full seasonal management.

WEEKLY MOWING & EDGING

Professional-grade mowing with sharp blades, clean edges along walkways and beds, and trimming around obstacles. We adjust mowing height seasonally to promote healthy grass growth and leave your lawn looking crisp every visit.

  • Consistent weekly schedule
  • String trimming & edging
  • Seasonal height adjustments

SPRING & FALL CLEANUPS

Spring cleanup removes winter debris, dead foliage, and matted leaves to prepare your landscape for the growing season. Fall cleanup clears leaves, cuts back perennials, and prepares beds for winter. Both are essential in Rochester's four-season climate.

  • Debris & leaf removal
  • Perennial cutbacks
  • Bed preparation

MULCHING & BED CARE

Fresh mulch suppresses weeds, retains moisture, and gives beds a polished appearance. We apply premium-quality mulch at the right depth and maintain clean bed edges throughout the season. Weed control — both chemical and hand-pulling — keeps your beds pristine.

  • Premium mulch installation
  • Bed edging & definition
  • Weed control

PRUNING & HEDGE TRIMMING

Proper pruning promotes healthy growth, maintains shape, and prevents disease. Our crews know when and how to prune each species — timing matters, especially for flowering shrubs. Hedge trimming keeps borders sharp and formal plantings looking their best.

  • Species-specific timing
  • Formal hedge shaping
  • Dead wood removal

SEASONAL COLOR ROTATIONS

Keep your property vibrant throughout the growing season with planned flower rotations. We install spring bulbs, summer annuals, and fall mums at the right time to ensure continuous color from April through October. Perfect for commercial properties and HOA common areas.

  • Spring, summer & fall rotations
  • Container planting
  • Entrance & focal point displays

IRRIGATION MANAGEMENT

For properties with irrigation systems, we provide spring activation, mid-season adjustments, and fall winterization. We also monitor for leaks, broken heads, and coverage gaps. Proper irrigation management saves water and keeps your landscape healthy through Rochester's variable summers.

  • Spring activation & testing
  • Seasonal adjustments
  • Fall winterization & blowout
