FAQ

COMMON QUESTIONS

How do I get a free estimate?

Fill out the form above, give us a call, or send an email. We'll schedule an on-site visit to assess your property and provide a detailed, no-obligation estimate within 48 hours.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the entire Greater Rochester area including Pittsford, Webster, Penfield, Fairport, Brighton, Greece, Gates, Chili, Henrietta, Victor, and surrounding communities.

Can I sign up for the 5-Step Lawn Care Program mid-season?

Yes! While the best results come from starting in spring, we can customize a program based on where you are in the season. Contact us and we'll recommend the best approach for your lawn.

Do you offer commercial services?

Absolutely. We serve HOAs, commercial properties, office parks, and retail locations with comprehensive landscape maintenance, snow management, and seasonal services. We'd be happy to discuss a custom commercial program.

Are your technicians certified?

Yes. All of our plant health and lawn care technicians are NYS DEC Certified Pesticide Applicators. Our company is fully licensed and insured.
