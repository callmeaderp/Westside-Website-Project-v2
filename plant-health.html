The Program

FIVE STEPS TO A PERFECT LAWN

Each application is precisely timed to match your lawn's growth cycle. Our NYS DEC Certified Applicators use professional-grade products for results you simply can't get from store-bought alternatives.

1
March — April

SPRING APPLICATION

Jump-start your lawn out of winter dormancy with a balanced fertilizer that promotes strong root development. Pre-emergent crabgrass control creates an invisible barrier that stops crabgrass seeds before they germinate. Broadleaf weed control targets any weeds that survived winter.

Balanced Fertilizer Pre-emergent Crabgrass Weed Control
2
May — June

LATE SPRING APPLICATION

A second round of balanced fertilization keeps your lawn thick and green as temperatures rise. Continued crabgrass control reinforces the spring barrier. Weed control targets new broadleaf weeds. Surface insect inspection identifies any emerging pest problems early.

Balanced Fertilizer Crabgrass Control Weed Control Insect Inspection*
3
June — July

SUMMER APPLICATION

The critical midsummer visit sustains growth during heat stress with balanced nutrition. Spot treatment addresses any crabgrass that broke through the barrier. Weed control continues to keep your lawn pristine.

Balanced Fertilizer Crabgrass Spot Treatment Weed Control Insect Inspection*
FREE
Guaranteed Grub Control
Merit application included with this visit (June/July). Prevents destructive grub damage all season — included at no extra charge.
4
August — September

LATE SUMMER APPLICATION

As summer winds down, this application prepares your lawn for the fall recovery period. Balanced fertilizer supports continued growth. Broadleaf weed control hits any late-season invaders. Surface insect inspection ensures no pest populations have established.

Balanced Fertilizer Broadleaf Weed Control Insect Inspection*
5
October — November

FALL APPLICATION

The final visit is arguably the most important. A winterizing fertilizer promotes deep root growth that will pay dividends in the spring. Spot weed treatment addresses any remaining broadleaf weeds before dormancy sets in.

Balanced Fertilizer Spot Weed Treatment Winterization

* Surface insect inspection/treatment as needed. Treatment applied when pest populations are detected at actionable levels.

ADDITIONAL SERVICES

Take your lawn to the next level with these add-on services, available individually or bundled with your 5-step program.

DEEP ROOT FERTILIZATION

Direct nutrient injection into the root zone of trees and shrubs for maximum vitality.

BED MAINTENANCE

Weekly weed control — both chemical and hand-picking — to keep your beds pristine all season.

AERATION

Core aeration relieves soil compaction, allowing water, nutrients, and air to reach grass roots.

OVERSEEDING

Premium grass seed application to fill in thin spots and improve lawn density and color.

LAWN REPAIR

Targeted repair of damaged areas from disease, insects, or wear and tear.

DETHATCHING

Mechanical removal of excess thatch to restore water and nutrient penetration.
